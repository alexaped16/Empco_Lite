Product Description

Model 400 ADA Audible Device

Part # 98-01930

Specification:

  • The ADA is an audible information device that can be mounted on various safety devices like the Safety Wall.
  • Easily record and program up to a sixty second message with built-in microphone and speaker.
  • Customize messages for each location. When routes are blocked or there are alternate crossings, these units provide positive guidance for the visually impaired by offering necessary audible information.
  • Powered by two six volt batteries installed in the base of the barricade light housing.
  • Unit is triggered by a motion detector when pedestrians get within fifteen feet of the housing.
  • Volume adjusts automatically with ambient noise.
  • Yellow base is standard, other colors available upon request.
