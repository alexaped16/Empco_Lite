Product Description

Wireless Channelizing Sequential Device

Part # 98-2012SMONO, 98-2012SSMON (Solar Assist)

Specification:

  • The devices are lit continuously, with a momentary flash that sequences through the series of lights.
  • Advanced warning helps to decrease vehicle speed and increase driver compliance in nighttime work zones.
  • Greatly increases early taper merges, studies also show increased cost benefits and effectiveness for improving safety in nighttime work zones.
  • Steady burn mode Meets current MUTCD and I.T.E standards tested by an approved certified laboratory.
  • Devices are synchronized via “Mesh” network using 2.4 GHz RF controlled circuits not requiring them to be in “eye sight” of each other and communicate up to 1,000 feet from each other automatically positioning themselves in the correct sequence.
  • Offered in solar and non-solar options.
  • Yellow base is standard, other colors available upon request.
