Product Description

Dredge Light

Part # 98-17400L

Specification:

  • Tested and certified by US Coast Guard accepted independent laboratory.
  • LEDs last 10,000 hours.
  • Steady burn light travels 2 nautical miles for up to 21 days.
  • Flashing light travels 2 nautical miles for up to 60 days.
  • Solid state circuitry turns light on at dusk and off at day-break.
  • Steady mode is available in red, green, and clear.
  • Flash mode is available in amber.
  • In steady mode the battery will last 20-25 days.
  • Flash mode will last 60 days.
  • Available with optional magnetic base.
