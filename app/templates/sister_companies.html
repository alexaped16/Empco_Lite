Sister Companies

Expertise, Technology, Quality ... since 1949

It all started with a few hard-working people with a vision, working in a small, garage-like, 750 square-foot structure. In 1949, Elgin Molded Plastics was born. Their goal was to produce the highest quality products on time, each an everytime. Dedication to excellence led to success, especially in the lighting industry. Empco-Lite was founded in 1968, making airline, construction, railroad, and marine lighting products available to customers. Once again, dedication to excellence led to success, and there was a need to create a company soley focused on highway/construction lighting. In 1978, AKT Construction was formed.

We believe in continuous improvement. We have been industry leaders for over 75 years because we have evolved with the times. We are very proud to be a woman-owned business. We also attribute our success to a dedication of excellence that our founders had. All of our products are made in America, right in our plant. We are a family-owned business, and believe that all of our customers should be treated like family too.
