Product Description

Concrete Barrier Wall Marker

Part # JD-1 (single side), JD-2 (double side)

Specification:

  • Dimensions: 3.89” X 4.15” X .76” (9.9 cm X 10.55 cm X 1.95 cm)
  • Slope of Reflecting Face: 32 degrees to base.
  • Area of Each Reflecting Face: 3.25 sq. in. (21 sq. cm.)
  • Lens is molded of methyl methacrylate conforming to ASTM D788 grade 8.
  • 10% of the content by weight is composed of post consumer recycled-content.
  • Thin untempered glass is bonded to the prismatic retroreflective faces to provide an extremely hard, durable, and abrasion resistant surface.
  • Available in: One way and two way, with white, amber, green, and blue options.
