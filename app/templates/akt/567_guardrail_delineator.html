Product Description

567 Guardrail Delineator

Part # 567-1 (single side), 567-2 (double side)

Specification:

  • Excellent visibility, even in rain, fog, sleet, and snow.
  • Fits comfortably within the center channel of guardrail protecting from accidental damage from snow plows.
  • Size: 5” X 2 1⁄4”
  • Effective Reflex Area: 8 sq. in.
  • Bracket is 12 gauge galvanized steel that attaches to the barrier with a bolt.
  • Available in No. 818, with a hole instead of prongs for the bracket.
  • Available in Amber, White, Red, and other colors by request.
  • Option of Engineer grade or High-intensity grade reflective sheeting.
