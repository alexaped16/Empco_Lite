Product Description

181 Guardrail Delineator

Part # 181-1 (single side), 181-2 (double side)

Specification:

  • Excellent visibility, even in rain, fog, sleet, and snow.
  • Mono-directional, only has a reflector on one side.
  • Ultrasonic welding permanently seals the reflector to the backing keeping out water, dirt, and dust for maximum reflectivity.
  • The reflector is riveted to the heavy angle 12 gauge galvanized steel bracket to prevent breakage.
  • Size: 3 1⁄4” diameter.
  • Effective Reflex Area: 7 sq. in.
  • Available in Amber or Crystal.
  • Available in No. 182, Bi-directional with reflectors on both sides of the steel bracket.
